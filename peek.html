Peek-Rutgers Honors College Online Magazine

The Honors College at Rutgers wanted to create an online platform, that allows Honors College students to showcase their talent. I received design plans from the design team, and worked to implement the designs. I was the only developer working on this project. The magazine is set to launch this year.
